  • New School Canteen

    The new canteen is been renovated and made more spacious to accomodate 3 times more number of students than earlier[150-175 sitting capacity]. The ventilation is improved for better air circulation. The food distribution counters are increased from 3-6. Food items served for breakfast and lunch are being designed with the help of the school dietician and more emphasis is given on the taste and nutritional value. In-house kitchen facility is built to serve freshly made hygenic food to the students and staff. Better kitchen waste disposal unit is built behind the kitchen.
